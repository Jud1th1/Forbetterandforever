Program Overview

Preparing Couples for a Lifetime of Commitment, Love and Growth

Marriage is one of life’s most meaningful commitments. It is a promise rooted in love, trust, and the hope of building a future together—through every season of life. For Better & For Ever® is a marriage preparation and enrichment program that helps couples begin that journey in mutual understanding with the support of others.

Grounded in Catholic tradition and wisdom, the program is designed to serve couples from all faith backgrounds, cultures, and life experiences, offering guidance for a lifelong commitment.
